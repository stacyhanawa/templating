Stacy Hanawa

Experienced Designer in the Architecture Industry

Creative Thinker | Problem Solver | Team Player



As a recent graduate from the University of California, Berkeley, I enjoy bringing both of my passions for architecture and sustainable design to the table. As a Bay Area native, I am fortunate to have grown up in such a diverse environment, constantly inspired to remain curious and to continue learning about the industry. As a project designer, I contribute to all phases of the design, from initial programming to construction documents.

“I would like to tap into this background of mine and contribute back to my local community, and have it serve as a stepping stone to becoming more involved in communities I have not previously been exposed to.”

I enjoy the hands-on nature of design, the process of refining aesthetics, and coming up with creative solutions, striving to create architecture that is an interplay of these various aspects of design.
